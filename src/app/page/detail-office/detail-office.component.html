Description

Miami state-of-the-art shared workspace for entrepreneurs, creative professionals, and small businesses. Our flexible workspace options are listed below: SEAT (from $249/month) Upgrade from your home office - or local Starbucks - without breaking the bank. Just grab any available SEAT at our communal worktables and get down to business. Includes high-speed connections, and access to a host of amenities such as meeting rooms, lockers, color printers, and more. From $199/month. STATION (from $449/month): Get your own spacious desk with large window in an open plan environment. Available for both individuals and small teams. Includes access to all of our amenities. STUDIO ($649/month): Enjoy a little more privacy and storage in our stylish and super-functional glass studios from Herman Miller. Includes access to all of our amenities. SUITE (from $1199/month): Get your own private office. Literally. Four walls, nice views, and some pretty sweet furnishings too. Available in different sizes and layouts for 1 - 4 people. Includes access to all of our amenities.
