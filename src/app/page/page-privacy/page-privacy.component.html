Information Collection and Use

Information Collection

Officelist, LLC is the sole owner of the information collected on Officelist.com. Officelist.com collects information from our users at several different points on our Web site. Business Centers and users alike enter information and are solely responsible for the veracity of the data entered. We will try to verify information but cannot guarantee its accuracy.

Registration

In order to access contact information for the locations that might serve them, users must first complete the registration form. During registration user is required to enter contact information such as company name, first and last name of contact, email address and phone number. We use this information to contact the user about services on our site for which he has expressed interest and to forward this information to Business Centers that might be able to service their specific needs. It is also required for the user to provide requirements information such as Monthly budget, Number of people, estimated length of occupancy and expected move in date. User is also required to select a unique password so we can provide a personalized experience on our site.

Search

We request information from the user on both our quick and advanced search forms. A user must provide information on the location of the Business Centers that might be considered for viewing so it can be referred to the appropriate locations.

Information use

Officelist.com gathers information to offer you the best available solution to your office needs. The site's main purpose is to find you temporary office space quickly, easily and for free. There is no charge for utilizing this site to find office space. Information gathered through the forms will be forwarded to the locations that might be able to provide you with service. Business Centers will then contact you to offer their services and compete for your business. We keep a record of all registrants to be able to follow-up on the progress of the referral. The site also keeps a log based on aggregated information which tells us about your experience reaching the site and while at it. Aggregated Information will be used exclusively by officelist.com. Collectively, this information helps us improve our service, the efficiency in our procedures and the effectiveness of our marketing efforts. This information is not cross-referenced with contact information entered.

Profile

We store information that we collect through cookies and log files to create a profile of our users. A profile is stored information that we keep on individual users that details their viewing preferences. Consequently, collected information is tied to the users' personally identifiable information to follow-up on progress and improve the content of the site for the user. This profile is used to tailor a user's visit to our Web site and to learn when a business is closed between the Business Center and a specific user. We do not share your profile with other third parties and listed Business Centers have agreed not to do so either.

Cookies

A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user's computer tied to information about the user. We use both session ID cookies and persistent cookies. For the session ID cookie, once users close the browser, the cookie simply terminates. A persistent cookie is a small text file stored on the user's hard drive for an extended period of time. Persistent cookies can be removed by following Internet browser help file directions. By setting a cookie on our site, users would not have to log in with a password more than once, thereby saving time while at our site. If users reject the cookie, they may still use our site. The only drawback to this is that the user will have to login every time they close their browser window and want to visit the site again. Some of our business partners may use cookies on our site. However, we have no access to or control over these cookies, once we have given permission for them to set cookies for advertising. This privacy statement covers the use of cookies by officelist.com only and does not cover the use of cookies by any partners.

Log files

Like most standard Web site servers we use log files. This includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, platform type, date/time stamp, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track user's movement in the aggregate, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. IP addresses, etc. are not linked to personally identifiable information.

Communications from the Site

Special Offers and Updates

We send all new members a welcoming email to verify password and username and to ensure the search results are available to users in their inboxes. Registered users will receive periodic follow-up emails to monitor the progress of the search. These communications will be done by email and phone. If we believe that a lease has been signed, we may contact the user by phone to confirm the agreement. If a user signs a lease with a business center and this lease expires, officelist.com will send an email as a courtesy to users reminding them of the advantages of finding temporary office space through the site. This is a one time communication.

Service Announcements

On rare occasions it is necessary to send out a strictly service related announcement. For instance, if our service is temporarily suspended for maintenance we might send users an email. Generally, users may not opt-out of these communications, though they can deactivate their account. However, these communications are not promotional in nature.

Costumer Service

We communicate with users on a regular basis to provide requested services and in regards to issues relating to their account or their search. Initial responses will be done via email. If a problem has not been solved or the email address is not valid, user may be contacted by phone to reach a successful conclusion to the service issue or to confirm a closing with a specific referred location.

If a user elects to use our referral service for informing a friend about our site, we ask them for the friend's name and email address.
